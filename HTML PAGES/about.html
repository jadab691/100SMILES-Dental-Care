About 100SMILES Dental Care

Located at 33 Fantail St. in Razole, we have been serving the community for over three decades. With more than 50 years of combined experience, our clinic provides friendly, reliable, and compassionate dental care for patients of all ages.

We offer a comprehensive range of services, from routine check-ups and cleanings to advanced dental implants, orthodontics, and cosmetic dentistry. Our focus is on preventive care, patient education, and ensuring a positive experience for everyone.

Now, we offer a new online booking system for convenient appointment scheduling and a free trial dental checkup for our valued clients.

Our New Dental Technologies

  • Digital X-Rays: High-resolution images with minimal radiation.
  • Intraoral Cameras: Detailed images to detect problems early.
  • Laser Dentistry: Minimally invasive treatments with faster healing.
  • CAD/CAM Technology: Precise dental restorations in a single visit.
  • 3D Printing: Accurate models, surgical guides, and custom appliances.
  • Teledentistry: Virtual consultations from the comfort of your home.
